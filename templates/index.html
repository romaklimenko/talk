How to start?
Click "Authentificate" below. Then authentificate and click "Authorize". Then add xmpptalk@appspot.com to your contact list and send message like .Home to choose a current calendar.
How to add an event to my Google Calendar?
Just send message with event name to xmpptalk@appspot.com.
How to change current calendar?
Just send to xmpptalk@appspot.com message like ". [calendar name]". I.e.:
You: . Work
xmpptalk: Current calendar switched to Work
